Twinkle Dental Care

Our clinic is specialised in advanced diagnostics and treatment of dental disorders. We guarantee comprehensive diagnostics and offer various forms of dental care, surgical procedures, and cosmetic dental services. We are a highly motivated team, all with very individual qualities and experience, and a high standard of patient care. We have advanced high levels of training and decades of experience and we constantly strive to provide the best treatment available using the most up to date tried and tested technology.
